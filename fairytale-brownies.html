process
Research & Inspiration

I begin by gathering inspiration and building a mood board that aligns with the campaign's theme, seasonality, and brand guidelines. This step helps establish visual direction, typography, color palette, and overall tone before moving into production.

Asset Design

Next, I design the core visual assets in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. This includes hero graphics, product callouts, promotional banners, and supporting visuals that will anchor the email layout.

Email Build in Klaviyo

Once assets are finalized, I export and upload them into Klaviyo, where I build out the full email layout. During this stage, I create buttons, links, and supporting design elements directly within the platform. I also implement light HTML customization when needed to refine spacing, alignment, or responsiveness.

Testing & Scheduling

Before launch, I review the email for visual consistency, functionality, and mobile responsiveness. After final approval, I schedule the campaign within Klaviyo to ensure timely and strategic delivery.
