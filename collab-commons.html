Collab Commons

overview

At The Collab Commons, I developed the brand from the ground up for this collaborative space dedicated to bringing creative minds together. I designed the logo, core brand identity, supporting assets, environmental graphics for both exterior and interior spaces, and cohesive brand collateral to ensure consistency across all touch points.

challenges

A key challenge was creating a brand that felt inspiring and inclusive of diverse creative disciplines while maintaining a strong, unified identity. Designing across both digital and physical environments required balancing creativity, functionality, and scalability.
